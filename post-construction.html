FAQ

What does post-construction cleaning entail?

Post-construction cleaning includes removing all traces of construction debris, clearing out construction dust, paint splatters, and all those leftovers. It's designed to prepare a new build or remodel for occupancy.

Are you equipped to handle large-scale construction cleaning?

Yes, we have the equipment and manpower to handle large-scale post-construction cleaning projects for both residential and commercial properties.

How do you dispose of construction debris?

We follow local regulations for the safe disposal of construction materials and debris. Our team ensures that all waste is handled responsibly and sustainably.

What safety measures do your cleaners take during post-construction cleans?

Safety is our top priority. Our cleaners wear personal protective equipment (PPE) and follow strict guidelines to ensure they safely handle construction residue and cleaning chemicals.

Post Construction

Post Construction Cleaning: Our comprehensive service is designed to address the unique challenges of post-construction clean-up, ensuring your space is spotless and move-in ready. This two-stage cleaning process begins with a rough clean, where our team conducts a thorough cleaning of all areas, including heavy broom cleaning and detailed vacuuming of all surfaces and trim. We remove small debris and trash, paying special attention to mechanical rooms, basements, and outdoor areas like garages and balconies.

In the final stage, we elevate the cleanliness to ensure no traces of dirt, dust, or construction debris remain. This includes a meticulous wipe-down of all trim and casing, and a repeat of our thorough floor and trim vacuuming. Our goal is to prepare your home or building perfectly, handling even the smallest details so that the space is pristine and welcoming. For a complete breakdown of tasks performed during each stage, please refer to the cleaning checklist provided at the end of this page.

Benefits of Post Construction

  • Removes construction debris, dust, and residues left behind after renovations or construction projects.
  • Improves safety by eliminating hazards such as loose nails or debris.
  • Restores the property to a clean and inhabitable condition.
  • Prepares the space for occupancy or final touches after construction work.
  • Customized cleaning solutions tailored to the specific needs of the project.
