FAQ

What is included in a deep cleaning service?

Deep cleaning involves a thorough clean of all areas of your home, including hard-to-reach places like top of cabinets, behind light weighted furniture, interior windows, inside the oven, and inside the refrigerators. It also includes detailed scrubbing of tiles, grout, and more extensive dusting of all surfaces.

How often should I schedule a deep cleaning?

We recommend scheduling a deep cleaning at least once every six months to maintain a high level of cleanliness in your home. It's especially beneficial during seasonal changes or before and after hosting significant events.

How long does deep cleaning take compared to regular cleaning?

Deep cleaning takes longer than regular cleaning due to the level of detail involved. The exact time will depend on the size of your home and its current condition, but it generally takes about twice as long as a standard cleaning session (about 4 hours).

Is there anything I need to do to prepare my home for a deep cleaning service?

To make the most of your deep cleaning service, we suggest clearing away personal items and decluttering surfaces. This allows our cleaners to focus on cleaning the home thoroughly without needing to spend time organizing or moving items.

Deep Cleaning

This is our signature service, a top-to-bottom, detail-oriented, rewarded cleaning. Our team will go to all the hidden spots that we don’t notice on a daily basis. This service also includes the inside of your appliances, inside of all reachable windows, and inside of your cabinets if your home is empty. For this service, we usually send a team of 4 cleaners but this number can vary and the amount of time we spend will depend on each individual project based on the property size, house organization, and actual cleaning conditions. 

Benefits of Deep Cleaning

  • Removes dirt, grime, and bacteria from hard-to-reach areas.
  • Improves indoor air quality by eliminating dust and allergens.
  • Restores the appearance and freshness of the space.
  • Helps prevent mold and mildew growth.
  • Ideal for thorough cleaning on a periodic basis.
