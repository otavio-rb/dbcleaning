Covid-19

At DB Cleaning Services, we understand the importance of maintaining a clean and safe environment. We recognize that the COVID-19 pandemic has brought about significant changes to the way we live and work “our new normal”. We are committed to ensuring that our cleaning services meet the highest standards of safety and hygiene and continue to prioritize your safety and the safety of our employees.

We have implemented a new cleaning service protocol that is designed to meet the evolving needs of our customers in a post-pandemic world. Our employees are equipped with the latest protective gear and cleaning products to ensure that your home or office is thoroughly cleaned and disinfected.

We understand that maintaining a clean and healthy environment is more important now than ever before. That’s why we continue to closely monitor developments and follow the advice of public health organizations to stay up-to-date on best practices for preventing the spread of COVID-19.

We are open for business and operating as an essential service under the Hygiene Products and Services Category. We have taken all necessary precautions to ensure that our employees are healthy and equipped with the proper protective gear, including masks and gloves, to keep themselves and our customers safe.
