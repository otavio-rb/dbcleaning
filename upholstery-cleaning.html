FAQ

What types of upholstery fabrics can you clean?

We clean a wide range of fabrics including synthetic fibers, cotton, wool, silk, and more. We adjust our cleaning methods based on the fabric type to ensure safe and effective cleaning.

How long does it take for upholstery to dry after cleaning?

Upholstery typically takes 2 to 6 hours to dry completely, depending on the fabric type and ventilation. We recommend using fans or air conditioning to speed up the drying process.

How often should upholstery be professionally cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every 12 to 24 months, depending on usage and exposure to dirt and allergens.

Can you remove pet odors and stains from upholstery?

Yes, our cleaning techniques are effective at removing pet odors and stains. We pre-treat areas with specialized solutions to ensure the best results.

Upholstery Cleaning

Upholstery Cleaning revitalizes and extends the life of your sofas, chairs, and other fabric-covered furniture. Our cleaning techniques remove dirt, dust, and allergens that accumulate in fabric fibers, restoring the original color and texture of your upholstery. Regular cleaning can prevent premature wear and tear, keeping your furniture looking new and inviting. This service not only improves the aesthetics of your furniture but also enhances the overall hygiene and air quality of your environment.

Benefits of Upholstery Cleaning

  • Extends the life of your furniture
  • Enhances the appearance of upholstery
  • Improves indoor air quality by removing allergens
  • Prevents buildup of dirt and grime
  • Restores fabric color and texture
