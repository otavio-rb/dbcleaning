Cleaning for a reason

We are proud to be a partner of Cleaning For a Reason, a distinguished nonprofit organization providing free home cleaning to cancer patients. At DB Cleaning Services, we understand the challenges faced during cancer treatment, and we are committed to offering compassionate, quality cleaning services to those in need. If you or a loved one are undergoing cancer treatment and would benefit from our services, we encourage you to apply through Cleaning For a Reason. Together, we strive to make a meaningful difference by giving you one less thing to worry about — a clean, comfortable home.

About Cleaning for a Reason

A Signature Charity of ISSA, the Worldwide Cleaning Industry Association

Mission

Cleaning for a Reason is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to helping cancer patients by providing free home cleaning services. The goal is to alleviate the burden of home maintenance, allowing patients and their families to focus on health and recovery.

Impact Since 2006
  • Patients Served: Over 46,000 cancer patients have been assisted through the program.
  • Value of Donated Services: The program has delivered cleaning services valued at more than $15.5 million.
  • Cleaning Company Partnerships: Collaboration with over 1,200 cleaning companies enhances the program's ability to serve.
Get Involved

Participation is open to cleaning professionals, industry members, and community volunteers who wish to support cancer patients. Contributions from every sector help sustain and expand the program's reach, ensuring continuous support for those in need.
