FAQ

What kinds of odors can your treatment remove?

Our odor control treatments can remove cigarette smoke, pet odors, cooking smells, and mustiness effectively.

How do you treat different types of odors?

Our odor control strategy employs trisodium phosphate (TSP) treatments to effectively neutralize odors at their source. Depending on the odor's nature and intensity, we may also use thermal fogging or enzyme treatments for comprehensive elimination.

Is the odor control treatment safe for fabrics and furniture?

Yes, our treatments are designed to be safe for all types of fabrics and surfaces within your home or business.

How long does the odor removal process take?

The process typically takes a few hours, depending on the size of the area treated and the severity of the odors.

Odor Control Treatment

Our Odor Control Treatment, featuring trisodium phosphate (TSP), tackles unpleasant smells directly at their source, achieving an effectiveness rate of 80 - 90%. Utilizing advanced technology paired with specially formulated products, we efficiently eliminate odors caused by pets, smoking, cooking, and mold. This service is indispensable for residential settings as well as commercial environments such as hotels and gyms, where maintaining a fresh and neutral air quality is paramount. Our treatments are safe for all types of fabrics and surfaces, ensuring that your space remains fresh, inviting, and free of any harmful residues.
