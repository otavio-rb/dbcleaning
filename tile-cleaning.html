FAQ

What types of tile can you clean?

We clean all types of tile, including ceramic, porcelain, slate, and natural stone. Each material requires specific cleaning solutions and techniques, which our technicians are fully equipped to handle.

How do you clean tile and grout?

We use high-pressure water and eco-friendly cleaners to remove dirt, grime, and bacteria from tile and grout. For tougher stains, specialized cleaning agents may be applied.

Can you restore the appearance of old tile?

Yes, our tile cleaning process can significantly restore the appearance of old and dull tiles, making them look bright and refreshed.

Is tile cleaning safe for my pets and family?

Absolutely. Our cleaning products are safe for homes with children and pets, and we ensure a thorough rinse and ventilation process to eliminate any residue.

Tile Cleaning

Tile Cleaning services rejuvenate your tiled floors and walls by removing grime, stains, and bacteria that build up over time. Our specialized cleaning solutions and techniques restore the original shine and color of your tiles, making them look brand new. Regular cleaning prevents mold and mildew growth, especially in moist areas like bathrooms and kitchens. It also enhances the durability of your tiles by removing abrasive dirt that can wear down grout and tile surfaces. This service is ideal for maintaining the cleanliness and aesthetic appeal of both residential and commercial spaces.

Tile Cleaning Benefits

  • Restores the original appearance of tiles
  • Prevents mold and mildew growth in damp areas
  • Enhances the durability of tiles and grout
  • Keeps tile surfaces hygienic and clean
  • Improves the overall aesthetic of the space
