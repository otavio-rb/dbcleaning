FAQ

What makes your green cleaning service different?

Our green cleaning service uses products that are biodegradable, non-toxic, and made from renewable resources. This ensures that your home or office is not only clean but also maintains air quality and reduces chemical exposure.

Are green cleaning products as effective as traditional ones?

Yes, the green cleaning products we use are rigorously tested to ensure they meet our high standards for effectiveness and safety. They effectively clean without leaving harmful residues.

Can green cleaning help with allergies?

Absolutely. Since green cleaning products contain fewer allergens and toxic chemicals, they can significantly reduce the triggers for people suffering from allergies and respiratory issues.

Is green cleaning more expensive than traditional cleaning?

While there can be a slight premium for green cleaning due to the cost of sustainable products, many clients find the health and environmental benefits well worth the extra investment.

Green Cleaning

Our Green Cleaning service keeps your space spotless while being kind to the planet. Using eco-friendly products, we make sure your home or office is not just clean, but healthy too. It’s perfect for anyone looking to reduce their environmental footprint without compromising on a thorough clean.

Benefits of Green Cleaning

  • Uses environmentally friendly cleaning products and methods.
  • Reduces exposure to harmful chemicals, promoting a healthier indoor environment.
  • Supports sustainability initiatives by minimizing the ecological footprint.
  • Safe for use around children, pets, and individuals with sensitivities or allergies.
  • Maintains high cleaning standards while prioritizing environmental responsibility.

What’s included in your Green Cleaning

Please note that this cleaning checklist can be adapted to better suit your needs.

All Rooms

  • Cobwebs removed
  • Dust top of door frames and door knobs
  • All light fixtures and fan blades dusted
  • All wall hangings dusted and cleaned
  • Dust decorative accessories
  • All glass and mirrors cleaned and sparkled
  • Floors vacuumed, swept, and mopped (if needed)
  • Empty wastebaskets and reline
  • Dust window blinds, sills and frames
  • Dust all baseboards, switch plates, and outlet covers
  • All furniture polished and dusted
  • Vacuum carpets and area rugs
  • Vacuumed under furniture
  • Empty wastebaskets and reline
  • Lamp and lampshades dusted

Kitchen

  • Outside cabinets and knobs dusted and spot cleaned
  • Tabletops, countertops, and backsplash cleaned and sanitized
  • Sinks and drain scrubbed, rinsed, and buffed
  • Stainless steel and chrome fixtures cleaned and shined
  • Range hood, exhaust fan, stovetop, and drip pans cleaned and sanitized
  • Clean sliding glass doors
  • Microwave oven cleaned inside and out
  • Exterior of small appliances wiped down (toaster, blender, etc.)
  • Clean front and top of refrigerator (where possible)
  • Garden window above sink spot cleaned
  • Place dirty dishes in dishwasher (no rinsing included)
  • Rinse big items, pots, and pans (don’t fit in dishwasher)

Bathrooms

  • All vanities, sinks, and countertops cleaned and sanitized
  • Outside cabinets and knobs dusted and spot cleaned
  • All stainless steel and chrome fixtures cleaned and sanitized
  • Toilets cleaned and sanitized, including base and rear
  • Tub and shower scrubbed, cleaned, and sanitized
  • Shower tile cleaned and sanitized
  • Clean tissue and towel holders
  • Change 1 set of towels (if provided and arranged)

Bedrooms

  • Vacuum under bed (if accessible)
  • Lamps and lamp shades dusted
  • All beds made only
  • Bed linens changed (if provided, 1 bed included only)

Utility Room

  • Exterior of washer and dryer cleaned
  • Utility sink cleaned and sanitized

Living Room

  • Vacuum under furniture cushions
  • Dust fireplace mantel
