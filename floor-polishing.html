FAQ

What types of floors do you polish?

We polish a variety of floor types including marble, granite, hardwood, and VCT (vinyl composition tile). Each type of flooring requires specific techniques and products to achieve the best results.

How often should floors be polished?

This depends on the type of flooring and the amount of foot traffic. Generally, polishing floors once or twice a year maintains their shine and protective finish.

What are the benefits of floor polishing?

Floor polishing not only improves the appearance of your floors but also adds a protective layer that helps prevent scratches and wear, extending the lifespan of the flooring.

How long does the polishing process take?

The time can vary based on the size of the area and the condition of the floor, but typically, polishing can take anywhere from a few hours to a full day.

Floor Polishing

Our Floor Polishing service brings the shine back to your hardwood, tile, and marble floors. Using state-of-the-art equipment and high-quality polishes, we gently buff away scratches and scuffs, leaving a high-gloss finish that transforms the look of any room. This service not only enhances the appearance of your floors but also adds a protective layer that can extend their lifespan. Regular floor polishing prevents the need for costly repairs and replacements, making it a smart investment for both the beauty and durability of your flooring. Ideal for both residential and commercial settings, our floor polishing service ensures your floors always look their best.
